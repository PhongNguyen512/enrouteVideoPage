FAQ

Can the ads be location and time programmed days, or months, or years in advance?

Yes, our DigiStream View ad provides this feature

Can the ads shown on the taxi top LED display be different from the ads shown on the inside taxi screen?

Yes. The inside taxi tablet and LED taxi topper units are independent from each other. You can choose to upload different ads and different formats (images or videos) independently.

What is the recommended duration for a video ad?

The industry recommended duration for a video ad is 20 seconds. This was set as a default but it can be changed by the user.

What is the recommended duration for a static image ad?

The industry recommended duration for static image ad is 10 seconds.

Are there updates and upgrades available?

Yes, whenever needed, the enRoute View Media team will provide software and hardware updates to its customers.

Can I use your DigiStream View with my own LED taxi top screens or my own inside taxi digital display tablet screen or monitors I purchased elsewhere?

Please note that we cannot guarantee the compatibility of our DigiStream View ad management platform with any generic LED taxi topper. We have specific hardware requirements for the LED taxi tops that need to be satisfied for proper operation of our DigiStream View. Please contact us for more details to discuss your customized options before you make your hardware purchase.

For the inside taxi tablet display, can the tablet’s built-in camera be disabled or removed?

Yes, we can customize the inside taxi tablet hardware and remove the built-in camera. Please contact us for more details to discuss your customized option.

Can the playing time of the customized video be adjusted to any duration?

Yes, it can. The default playing time is set to 20 seconds. However, DigiStream view management software offers the option of adjusting the length of the play time of the video by simply entering the time in default status when you upload the video.

When uploading my ads, do I need to keep the LED taxi top or the inside taxi tablet display screen turned on?

No, the LED taxi top or the inside taxi tablet display can be either on or off. The DigiStream View ad management system uploads the ads to the enRoute View Media cloud serve which is accessed by the LED taxi topper.

Can I edit the size of the geofenced region?

Your assigned global geofenced region (country, or geographical region, territory, state, province, etc.) is set by enRoute View Media when you initially subscribe for the service. You will be allowed to edit and set areas within this assigned region. If you like your global geofenced region to be edited or modified, you only need to provide the information of your new global geofenced region to the enRoute View Media team and they will do it for you.

Can I edit the default cities to include the rest of the country’s cities and regions?

If you wish to add additional cities and regions, you only need to contact enRoute View Media and they will be added to your region of operation.
